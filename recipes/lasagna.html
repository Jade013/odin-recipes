Lasagna Recipe

Home Lasagna Spaghetti Buffalo Wings

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, baked to perfection for a hearty and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked; drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by half of the ricotta mixture, half of the ground beef, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
