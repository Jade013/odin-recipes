Spaghetti Recipe

Home Lasagna Spaghetti Buffalo Wings

Description

This is a classic spaghetti recipe that is easy to make and delicious to eat. Perfect for a family dinner or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the sauce. Toss well to combine.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
