Buffalo Wings Recipe

Home Lasagna Spaghetti Buffalo Wings

Description

Buffalo wings are a popular American appetizer made from deep-fried chicken wings coated in a spicy sauce, typically served with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (like Frank's RedHot)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery sticks for serving
  • Blue cheese dressing for dipping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the wings with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Place the wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake the wings for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are crispy and golden brown.
  6. While the wings are baking, prepare the sauce by melting the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  7. Add the hot sauce, white vinegar, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the melted butter. Stir until well combined and heated through.
  8. Once the wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and place them in a large bowl.
  9. Pour the sauce over the wings and toss until they are evenly coated.
  10. Serve the buffalo wings hot with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing on the side.
